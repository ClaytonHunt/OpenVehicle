# ADR-001: Chassis Material and Structural Tubing Specification

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-04-08

## Context

The platform needs a structural approach that is practical for community fabrication while retaining conservative structural assumptions appropriate for full-size road-going use.

## Decision

1. **Primary structural members**
   - 1"x2" rectangular steel tubing
   - Wall thickness: **0.095" minimum**, **0.120" preferred** in higher-load regions

2. **Secondary members**
   - 1"x1" square tubing
   - Wall thickness: **0.095" recommended**

3. **High-load zones**
   - Use reinforced boxed structures, gussets, doubler plates, and/or thicker wall sections where required.

4. **Occupant protection structures**
   - Roll hoops/cages and related occupant-protection structures must use **round DOM or seamless tubing only**.
   - **ERW is not allowed** in roll structures.

5. **Body structure role**
   - Body panels are **non-structural** and shall not be credited with carrying primary chassis loads.

## Consequences

- Simplifies procurement and fabrication for most builders.
- Supports straight-cut fabrication workflows.
- Requires careful joint and load-path design in high-load areas due to mixed section sizes.
- Requires separate material/process controls for occupant-protection tubing.
